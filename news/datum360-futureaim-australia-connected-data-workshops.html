Datum360 & FutureAim Australia Connected Data Workshops

28.05.20 News & Events

Following the continued easing of lockdown restrictions in Australia relating to the Covid-19 pandemic, with everyone's safety in mind, we are delighted to announce new dates for our workshops for October.

We are fortunate and grateful to have the continued support of our original speaker line-up, meaning we can bring you the same quality workshops we had planned earlier this year, with speakers from BHP, South32 Worsley Alumina, OZ Minerals and FutureAim.

New dates for your calendar

  • Perth, Tuesday 20th October
  • Brisbane, Thursday 22nd October

We will be sending event reminders a little closer to the workshop dates with more details for those people already registered for the original events in March.
